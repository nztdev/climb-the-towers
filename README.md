https://nztdev.github.io/climb-the-towers/

Eternal MMO climbing progression game. 
Climb up forever. 
Every tower is shared: you see other players above and below you. You can leave rope bridges for others, or cut them. 
It's all saved. Your highest floor. Bridges you built. Messages you carved. 

Each version generated by an AI model. So far CGPT, Gemini, Kimi & Claude. Pick your favourite!
